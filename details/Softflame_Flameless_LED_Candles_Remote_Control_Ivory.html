Softflame Flameless LED Candles with Remote Control

Add a touch of warmth and elegance to your living space without the hazards of real candles. These flameless LED candles are designed to bring a realistic 3D flame effect to any room, creating a cozy and inviting atmosphere for your home or special occasions.

Safety First, Beauty Second

Unlike traditional candles, Softflame flameless candles operate on batteries, eliminating the risk of fire, smoke, and messy wax. This makes them an excellent choice for families with children or pets, ensuring a safe and worry-free experience.

Control Your Ambiance

With a convenient remote control, you can easily adjust the brightness and flickering effect of these candles to suit your mood or occasion. Choose from 6 timer settings and 2 brightness levels to set the perfect mood for dinners, parties, or tranquil evenings.

Realistic Flame Design

Each candle features a lifelike 3D flame that moves naturally, just like a real candle. The design enhances the visual appeal of any room, making your interior decor more inviting and stylish.

Long-Lasting Performance

Enjoy up to 700 hours of use with just 2 C batteries. These flameless candles are built to last, making them a cost-effective and eco-friendly option for your everyday lighting needs.

Reversible for Reuse

The reversible design allows you to use these candles multiple times, simply flipping them over to access the other side. This versatility makes them a great addition to any seasonal decor setup.

Whether you're aiming for a calming ambiance or a romantic glow, these indoor decor candles are perfect for any setting. Use them to set the tone for your dinner table, office, or living room and enjoy the comfort of a safe, stylish lighting solution.

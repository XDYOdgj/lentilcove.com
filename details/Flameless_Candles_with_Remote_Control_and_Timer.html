Realistic Flameless LED Candles with Remote Control and 24-Hour Timer

Experience the soft glow of real candles without the risks of open flames. These flameless candles use advanced flickering technology to mimic the gentle dance of a real flame, creating a warm and inviting atmosphere for your home, parties, weddings, or holidays. Safety is key, especially when you have children or pets around, making these candles an ideal choice for any setting.

With a built-in timer, you can set the candles to turn on automatically at your desired time and choose from lighting durations of 2, 4, 6, or 8 hours. The remote control allows you to adjust and manage multiple candles from a distance, making it easy to customise your lighting to match your event or mood.

Each candle is powered by 2 AA batteries (not included), and with high-quality batteries, you can expect up to 200 hours of usage. The LED bulbs are designed for long-lasting performance, with a lifespan of up to 50,000 hours. These decorative candles are compact, measuring 3" in diameter and 4" in height, making them perfect for tabletops, mantels, or any surface where ambiance is desired.

Whether you're hosting a celebration, setting the mood for a special evening, or simply wanting to add a touch of elegance to your home decor, these candles offer a safe and reliable alternative. They are a perfect blend of functionality and style, bringing a cozy feel without the hassle of real candles.

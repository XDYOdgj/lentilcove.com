Prairie Swag Curtains for Farmhouse and Country Cottage Decor

These elegant prairie swag curtains by Piper Classical bring a warm, vintage charm to your bathroom or window. Inspired by rural aesthetics, they are crafted to complement rustic and boho decor with their natural color palette and soft floral patterns.

Featuring small-scale flower clusters in rich brown, honey gold, and buttermilk tones, the mustard and yellow floral prints stand out beautifully against the soft white quilted background. The result is a soft yet eye-catching look that feels both timeless and fresh.

Made from lightweight cotton fabric, these curtains offer comfort and breathability, making them ideal for warmer months. The cotton lining adds a refined touch, ensuring they look as good as they perform while enhancing durability.

Perfect for spring, summer, or fall decor, these curtain swags are designed to fit seamlessly into your countryside or rustic home. Each set includes two mirrored swags with adjustable drawstring pulls, allowing you to customize the amount of coverage and hang them with ease.

You can hang them using the rod pocket or the fabric loops on the back, giving you two versatile options for installation. While dry cleaning is recommended to maintain their shape and color, they can also be machine washed and dried carefully for long-lasting use.

Whether you're looking to elevate your bathroom design or add a stylish twist to your window treatment, these prairie swag curtains are the perfect blend of functionality and vintage appeal.
